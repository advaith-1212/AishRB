# Cover Letter Rules

## Format
- 4 short paragraphs, 1 page max
- Professional but personal tone
- No corporate cliches

## Content
- Do NOT restate resume bullets
- Use specific examples showing skills the employer wants
- Keywords from the job posting, used naturally
- Never fabricate experiences or metrics

## Structure
1. Quick hook: role + company + why it caught your eye (2-3 sentences)
2. One story showing a skill the JD cares about
3. Why THIS company specifically, or another relevant story
4. Brief closing with availability
