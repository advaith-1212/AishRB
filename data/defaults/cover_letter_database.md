## Format (Cornell Tech Standard)

1. **Header:** Full name (center, bold, uppercase), contact line (city | phone | email only)
2. **Date:** Current date, left-aligned
3. **Salutation:** "Dear [Hiring Manager's Name]," (look up on LinkedIn; fallback "Dear Hiring Manager,")
4. **Paragraph 1:** Hook with role, company, and why specifically this company
5. **Paragraph 2:** Story 1 with metrics and impact
6. **Paragraph 3:** Story 2 showing a different skill
7. **Paragraph 4:** Values tied to company mission
8. **Paragraph 5:** Closing with availability
9. **Signature:** "Sincerely," + name + email
